Garfield's Dank Ass Lasagna

For this recipe, we are essentially making a thick, meaty tomato sauce and layering that with noodles and cheese into a casserole. Indulge your taste buds with a mouthwatering homemade lasagna that will leave you craving for more. This classic Italian dish is a symphony of flavors and textures. Here's the run-down:

Here's the list of ingredients you'll need for this recipe

  • 2 teaspoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef chuck
  • 1/2 medium onion, diced (about 3/4 cup)
  • 1/2 large bell pepper (green, red, or yellow), diced (about 3/4 cup)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28-ounce)can good-quality tomato sauce
  • 3 ounces tomato paste (half a 6-ounce can)
  • 1 (14 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh oregano, or 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley (preferably flat leaf), packed
  • 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 pinch garlic powder and/or garlic salt
  • 1 tablespoon red or white wine vinegar
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 tablespoon to 1/4 cup sugar (to taste, optional)

Now here's how we do this shit

  1. Boil Water and cook lasagna noodles until al dente
  2. Brown the ground beef
  3. Cook the bell peppers, onion and garlic until tender
  4. Add the ground beef to the cooked vegetables and cook for another 5 minutes
  5. Make the sauce by adding the crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste
  6. Add seasonings and beef and vegetable mixture
  7. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees
  8. Combine ingredients in baking dish by making layers of noodle, sauce, and ricotta
  9. Cook for 45 minutes covered, 10 minutes uncovered to brown the top layer
  10. Serve!
