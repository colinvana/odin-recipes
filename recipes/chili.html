Chili

Colin's Locally Famous Chili

Get ready to have your taste buds dancing with joy! I have an incredible chili recipe that will leave you craving for more. Picture this: a rich and hearty blend of flavors that will warm your soul and ignite your senses.

Ingredient list:

  • 1 lb Ground Beef or Impossible Meat
  • 1 Yellow Onion
  • 1 Red Onion
  • 8 oz Crushed Tomatoes
  • 14 oz Diced Tomatoes
  • 1 Can Black Beans
  • 1 Can Pinto Beans
  • 1 Bottle of Yeungling Beer or Lager
  • 1 tbs Worcestershire
  • Salt, Pepper, Chili Pepper, Cayenne, Paprika, Garlic Powder, Onion Powder

Step by Step Guide

  1. Cook ground beef or impossible meat until brown
  2. Chop onions and add into ground beef with spice mixture
  3. Cook until onions are translucent
  4. Add in tomatoes, beans, Worcestershire, and beer
  5. Bring to a boil
  6. Reduce heat to simmer when chili reaches boil
  7. Cook 45 minutes to an hour
  8. Top with sour cream and cheese and serve
